How to Become a Millionaire

Not a desire for status or a lavish lifestyle, but rather for financial security should be the driving force behind the quest of money. A seven-digit net worth is a reachable objective if you start young and form the appropriate financial habits. Jason Flurry, CFP, founder and president of Legacy Partners Financial Group in Woodstock, Georgia, has discovered in his work with wealthy clients that those he refers to as "true millionaires," people who acquire wealth and maintain it, view the role of money in their lives very differently from those who place more emphasis on what money can buy. "Being rich" or having money for the sake of having money never makes a person feel content, according to him. Ironically, it can result in a different set of issues for the majority of people.
